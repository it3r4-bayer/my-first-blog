    01  Hanging Out With The Boys
    Need some bonding time with your guy pals? Men tend to get closer as friends the more they hang out and participate in a variety of activities, especially when they feel physically or intellectually challenged in some way. Things like trivia nights, basketball games, and even a Sunday morning jog are all good choices for guy firend because they allow for bonding time and also time fo that competitive side to come through.
